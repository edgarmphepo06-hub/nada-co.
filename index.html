About NADA

A Vision Born from Passion and Innovation

Our Story

NADA (Next Level Advanced Digital Administration) was founded on June 6, 2024, by Edgar Mphepo, a visionary entrepreneur and technology enthusiast. The idea was conceived while Edgar was studying at William Murray Secondary School in Form 3, where he developed a passion for technology and digital innovation.

What started as a dream in the classroom evolved into a comprehensive multi-technology company, officially announced on June 18, 2024. NADA represents the culmination of years of dedication, learning, and the pursuit of excellence in the digital space.

Today, NADA stands as a testament to what can be achieved through determination, innovation, and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the digital world.

Edgar Mphepo

Founder & CEO

Edgar Mphepo is a passionate technology entrepreneur with a vision to revolutionize the digital landscape. His journey in technology began at William Murray Secondary School, where he discovered his love for digital innovation.

Edgar pursued his passion further at Exploits University - Mzuzu Campus, where he earned a Diploma in ICT. This academic foundation, combined with his natural entrepreneurial spirit, laid the groundwork for what would become NADA.

With a commitment to excellence and a drive to create meaningful technological solutions, Edgar continues to lead NADA with the same passion and determination that started it all.
